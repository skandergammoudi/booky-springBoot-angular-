Why use booki?

Manage, follow the list of your books to read is now easy thanks to Booki which makes it possible to regroup your next readings, to follow your progress and to take up challenges.

Books

A list of the best-selling books of the moment is recommended for you to help you choose your next readings.

Favorites

You can group your next readings in a pleasantly presented list in order to to facilitate the visualization and the management of your books to read.

Challenges

As soon as you finish reading a book, you can mark it as completed, and immediately start to take up the challenges present on Booki.
